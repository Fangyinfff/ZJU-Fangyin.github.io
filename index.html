Short Bio

I am presently in the third year of my doctoral program (since 2020) in Computer Science at Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Huajun Chen, Prof. Ningyu Zhang and Prof. Xiaohui Fan. My research pursuits are primarily in the fascinating domain of AI4Science. I am passionate about exploring the interplay between artificial intelligence and scientific inquiry, as well as its potential applications in natural language processing.

I am currently looking for a post-doc position in the field of AI4Science/NLP/KG. Feel free to reach me if you are interested in my research!
